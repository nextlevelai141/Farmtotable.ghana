Who we are

Ghana's leading healthy
ready-to-eat brand

The African Dream Farm and Food Services Limited is Ghana's leading fruit and vegetable processor, providing healthy ready-to-eat meals to health-conscious patrons across Accra since 2021.

We offer monthly and weekly meal programs for weight management, curated by our in-house nutritionists. We are proudly operated by women — delivering life-changing nutrition every single day.

We are the #1 breakfast provider on Bolt Food and Yango in Accra, having served over 1 million meals to more than 1,000 committed clients.
